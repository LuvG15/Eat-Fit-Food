About Us

Eat Fit Food provides the consumer with a plethora of healthy food choices, such that they have an alternative for every traditional food item thus providing to our customers a variety of options to choose from.

Our Story.

India’s first (probably the world’s first) entirely grain free and naturally sweetened lifestyle. Eat Fit Food’s endeavor is to change the way people eat, by indulging them with food such as burgers, pizzas, wraps and sandwiches made tasty and healthy. The kitchen uses 100% no grains, no wheat, no corn, no soya or rice, no preservatives, no chemicals, no refined oil/food, no additives, no bleach and no food colorings. Eat Fit Food foods are nutrient dense, packed with fiber, ‘good for you’ fats, carbs, proteins, superfoods, whole foods and real foods. The journey to get Sorted. began with the usual concerns with health and healthy eating particularly.

A Few Words About Us

Serving healthy alternative to your favorite foods.

Eat Fit Food’s flagship store is a fresh monochromatic space, sprawling across 3300 square feet divided into two sections, an indoor and outdoor that can seat up to a 100 people. The display counters with pastries, breakfast & dessert jars, cookie jars, breads, meats & cheese showcase just some of the culinary offerings. The ‘meet our ingredients’ board is informative and makes a great backdrop. Eat Fit Food has an open kitchen, where all the food, sauces, accompaniments and garnishes are made in house, using the most premium local and imported ingredients. The decor is simple with a contemporary theme, jazz music and lots of natural light add to the vibe.

Our Mission

To inspire healthier communities achieve their optimum health by connecting people to real food. We seek to transform 10 million lives across India over the next ten years.
